Privacy policy

This privacy policy governs your use of the software application "aria2 for Android". The Application is a front-end (pluggable graphical user interface) for aria2 BitTorrent client (which is also included in distribution).

Using aria2 to download files from Internet causes your device to contact a number of parties:

  • A number of specialized intermediary servers (also known as "torrent trackers");
  • One or more of file-serving servers (in case of HTTP downloads and torrents with web-seeds);
  • Unpredictable number of network hosts, some of which may participate in file exchange.

Those parties can gather some information about your device, your IP address, detect that your are using aria2, learn names and cryptographic hashes of files being transferred etc.

All of above are integral features of network and file-sharing protocols, used by aria2. aria2 for Android does not collect or distribute any information about yourself, your device, downloaded files or any other information except for the purpose of implementing those protocols.

Any collection, transfer or exchange of that information happens automatically as part of your use of aria2 for Android. Your use of the Application is treated as an explicit consent for that.

Some information may be collected by Google as part of using their services, including Google Services and Google Play Store. That information is subject to Google's privacy policy.

Depending on your use of aria2 it may store some information on your device, including:

  • Information about previously contacted network peers (in file "dht.dat")
  • Information about ongoing sessions
  • Cached meta-data of torrents, downloaded by following "magnet" links
  • Downloaded files themselves.

Depending on aria2 configuration, some of those files may be stored on storage accessible to other applications and other parties. Their actions towards that information is not subject to this privacy policy and aria2 for Android developers do not take any responsibility ###for them.

You can stop all collection of information by the Application easily by uninstalling the Application. You may use the standard uninstall processes as may be available as part of your mobile device or via the mobile application marketplace or network.
